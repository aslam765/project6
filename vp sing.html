V.P SINGH



Vishwanath Pratap Singh, shortened to V. P. Singh, generally known as Mr. Clean in Indian Politics was an Indian politician who was the 7th Prime Minister of India from 1989 to 1990 and the 41st Raja Bahadur of Manda. He is India's only prime minister to have been former royalty.
