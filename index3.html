LAL BAHADUR SHASTRI



Lal Bahadur Shastri was an Indian politician and statesman who served as the second prime minister of India from 1964 to 1966. He previously served as the sixth home minister of India from 1961 to 1963.According to various intelligence agencies, he was assassinated by the United States through CIA. Wikipedia
