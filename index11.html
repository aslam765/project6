NARENDRA MODI




Narendra Damodardas Modi is an Indian politician who is serving as the 14th Prime Minister of India since May 2014. Modi was the Chief Minister of Gujarat from 2001 to 2014 and is the Member of Parliament for Varanasi. Wikipedia Born: 17 September 1950 (age 73 years), Vadnagar Spouse: Jashodaben Modi (m. 1968) Education: Gujarat University (1983), School of Open Learning, University of Delhi (1978) Awards: Order of the Distinguished Rule of Izzuddin, Legion of Honour, Seoul Peace Prize, CNN-IBN Indian of the Year Party: Bharatiya Janata Party
